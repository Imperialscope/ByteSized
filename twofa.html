Two‑Factor Authentication (2FA)

1) Analogy: Two Locks

Your front door has a key, but you also use a passcode on the alarm. Even if someone copies your key, they still can’t get in without the second code. That’s 2FA.

2) What Counts as 2FA?

  • App codes (Authenticator, 1Password, Authy) — best balance of security and ease.
  • Security keys (YubiKey, passkeys) — strongest option.
  • SMS codes — better than nothing, but weaker than app codes.

3) Turn On 2FA (Example: Google)

1Go to myaccount.google.com → Security → “2‑Step Verification”.

2Choose an Authenticator app; scan the QR; enter the 6‑digit code.

3Save backup codes safely. Add an extra method like a security key.
